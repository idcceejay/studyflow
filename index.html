💾 Backup & Export

Your notes live only in this browser. Export a backup file to keep them safe, restore after clearing browser data, or move to another computer.

Tip: the ⬇ button at the top of any note downloads just that note as a PDF.

🧩 Combine notes into a study guide

Select the notes to combine. AI will merge them into one organized master study guide — perfect for a unit test or final exam. The guide becomes a regular note, so you can run Learn mode and flashcards on it too.
